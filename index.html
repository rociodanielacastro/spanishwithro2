about me

Spanish Coach for Real-Life Conversation

Spanish Coach for Real-Life Conversation

  • Hi, I’m Rocío — a Spanish coach from Argentina. I help students go from understanding Spanish to actually speaking it with confidence in real-life situations. My approach is conversation-first: you’ll speak from day one, using practical, everyday Spanish — with just enough structure to guide your progress without overwhelming you with theory. The goal isn’t perfection. It’s confidence, clarity, and being able to express yourself naturally.
